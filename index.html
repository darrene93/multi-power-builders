Company Overview

Multi Power Builders Pte Ltd is a BCA-registered SME contractor established in 2017, specializing in integrated building construction and electrical installation services across Singapore. With over 7 years of industry experience, we have successfully completed more than 500 projects ranging from commercial waterproofing and structural repairs to sophisticated fiber optic installations and building automation systems.

Our unique value proposition lies in providing comprehensive, integrated solutions that combine building works with electrical installation capabilities. This approach eliminates coordination complexity, reduces project timelines, and ensures seamless execution from design through completion.
