LTA Sky Camera (Solar) - Detailed Project

Project Overview:
MULTI POWER BUILDERS PTE. LTD. partnered with the Land Transport Authority (LTA) to provide a sustainable and reliable outdoor surveillance system, featuring advanced **IP PTZ cameras** powered by **solar energy** for high-exposure traffic zones. This project showcases our expertise in combining state-of-the-art technology with environmentally conscious solutions.

Scope of Work:
We were responsible for designing and implementing the complete surveillance infrastructure, which included the installation of **solar-powered IP PTZ cameras** for 24/7 monitoring of critical traffic zones.

Challenges & Solutions:
The main challenges included designing the system for harsh outdoor conditions and ensuring the cameras were powered sustainably. We overcame these by integrating **solar power systems** and using weather-resistant technology.

Results:
The project not only enhanced **traffic monitoring** but also reduced energy costs by using **solar power**, making it a sustainable solution. It has improved the safety and efficiency of road traffic management in high-exposure zones.
